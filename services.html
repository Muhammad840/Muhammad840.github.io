    Extensive hotel offerings

    Whether your clients are looking for a romantic getaway or a rich family adventure, we know a place. Our veteran account managers are ready to work with you to craft the most amazing itineraries your clients will ever experience. After all, this is the land of the pharaohs!

    Relationships That Pay

    Our long and productive relationships with properties across Egypt, along with the strength of our balance sheet, means that we are afforded credit terms that very few other providers can receive. In turn, we pass those flexible payment terms to you, ensuring that your capital is used as efficiently as possible, and not tied up with properties across the country.

    Ground handling

    24/7 Emergency support

    We can re-book flights, notify the hotel, arrange for ground transportation, and have a representative on the way before your clients leave their room.

    Airport-licensed meet and greet staff

    Our Meet-and-Greet representatives are licensed by the Egyptian Airports Authority to cross passport control and greet your travelers as soon as they arrive at the gate.

    Exclusive English and mandarin conceirge service

    Now your clients can head into town, equipped with a Menatours prepaid mobile phone, and whether they need a ride, restaurant reservations, or just an instant translation from English or Mandarin to Arabic, the Menatours Concierge Service is at their fingertips.

    Traveler materials and documentation

    Our ground handling team will make sure your materials are printed and ready to go before your travelers arrive, making sure that your brand is always present with your clients.

    The Menatours Signature Collection

    Our most exclusive services are available to you and your travelers as part of the Menatours Signature Collection. Over the years, our experiences and networks have allowed us access to privileges not available to your everyday travelers. As experts in luxury travel, the Menatours Signature Collection of services is a compilation of experiences usually reserved for visiting dignitaries, ranging from exclusive tours guided by world-renowned faculty to private access to some of the world’s most awe-inspiring sites.
