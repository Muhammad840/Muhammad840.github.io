    About Menatours

    Established in 1956, Menatours is one of the oldest DMCs in Egypt.

    Our history

    Founded in 1956 by Egyptian shipping magnate and entrepreneur Mohamed Leheta, Menatours is the 49th registered tour operator in Egypt, and has since commanded a leading role in the tourism industry in Egypt.

    As our world becomes more dynamic everyday, our heritage and history at Menatours compels us to continually evolve through creative frameworks that allow us to leverage our experience in adapting to and exceeding client expectations.

    Mohamed Leheta Founder, 1921-2012

    Our leadership

    Menatours’ executive leadership team features more than 150 years’ combined experience in all facets of global travel, hospitality, logistics, technology, and the law. They have set a winning strategy that our clients can count on to help them keep moving and deliver travel management services that drive bottom line results .

    Farouk Nasser

    Chairman & chief executive officer

    Dr. Farouk Nasser serves as Chairman of the Board of Directors and Chief Executive Officer of Menatours, and brings extensive management experience to the Menatours leadership team. Dr. Nasser also serves as Chairman and Managing Director of International Development Consultants , an investment banking and private equity office with over 30 years of experience in Egypt and the Middle East. Dr. Nasser also serves as Chairman of the Sinai White Cement Company, a publicly traded corporation in Egypt, and on the board of several of Egypt’s largest corporations including fertilizer manufactures, construction materials producers and private schools.
    Dr. Nasser holds a B.Sc. degree in Engineering from Cairo University , M.Sc. & PhD. in Engineering from the USA as well finance and economic studies. He attended University of Cincinnati U.C. , Berkeley and Notre Dame.

    Khaled Fawzy

    Chief operations officer

    Khaled Fawzy servers as the Chief Operations Officer at Menatours, and reports directly to the Cheif Executive Officer. Khaled brings over 30 years of travel industry experience. Prior to joining the Menatours Leadership team, Khaled was Cheif of Operations for Thomas Cook in Egypt, overseeing over 11 locations throughout the country. Prior to that, Khaled worked with some of the largest travel services providers in Egypt.
    Currently, Khaked leads over 100 employees throughout Menatours, including all of our 18 locations throughout the country. Khaled holds a B.A. in Commerce from Cairo Univeristy.

    Ahmed El-Waraky

    Chief financial officer

    Ahmed El Waraky serves as Chief Financial Officer at Menatours, and reports directly to the Chief Executive Officer.
    Ahmed has been a Menatours veteran for most of his career. Ahmed started with Menatours in 1989 as a Junior Accountant, and quickly moved up to Accounting Manager. Shortly afterwards, Ahmed was assigned to the Menatours’ Internal Audit Department, and soon after moved forward to lead the Internal Audit Department in 2004. Finally, in 2009, Ahmed was appointed as Chief Financial Officer at Menatours, and continues to be instrumental to the Menatours Leadership team.
    Ahmed holds a B.A in Commerce from Cairo University, and was formerly President of the National Development Committee of Giza, a non-profit NGO.

    Mahmoud Maher

    General counsel

    Mahmoud serves as the General Counsel of Menatours, and reports directly to the Chief Executive Officer. Mahmoud brings over 15 years of experience, having focused on commercial and arbitration law. Prior to joining Menatours, Mahmoud was part of the leadership team at multiple prominent law firms in Cairo. Mahmoud is registered with the Egyptian Bar Association and is a registered Appellate lawyer, licensed to practice across all governorates in Egypt.
    Mahmoud holds a B.A. and J.D. from Cairo University.

    Client Retention is key at Menatours

    Throughout our history as a company, we have always invested more heavily in retaining existing clients than in bringing on new ones. The reason behind that is simple: our strategy of calls for superior customer satisfaction, which means we only bring on new customers when we now we can add value to their operations. This philosophy has served as well through the years, and has led some of our client relationships to last more than 40 years.
